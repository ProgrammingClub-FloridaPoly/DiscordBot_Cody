**SERVER RULES**

**1. Follow the Discord Community Guidelines and Terms Of Service: https://discordapp.com/guidelines**
Florida Poly Students must also follow the Student Code of Conduct: https://floridapoly.edu/student-affairs/student-development/student-handbook-code-of-conduct.php

**2. Be respectful. Do not be disruptive.**
Respect all student members, guests, & bots, regardless of your liking towards them. Treat others the way you want to be treated.

**3. No Direct & Indirect Threats.**
Threats to other users of DDoS, Death, DoX, abuse, and other malicious threats are absolutely prohibited and disallowed.

**4. No Inappropriate Language or Materials.**
The use of profanity should be kept to a minimum. However, any derogatory language towards any user is prohibited. Also, all NSFW materials are stricly prohibited. We must maintain professionalism.

**5. Keep discussions relevant to club server, channel topics, and guidelines.**
There are specific channels that will allow you to discuss certain topics. If you are not sure, just ask. 

**6. No blank, invisible, or offensive names/nicknames. No offensive profile pictures.**
You will be asked to change your name or picture if they are deemed inappropriate.

**7. All members shall have limited access until verified as Florida Poly Students. Nicknames shall be maintained in the following format: firstName + last initial (ex: Cody P.)**
You will lose certain roles/privileges on this server.

**8. Do not provide or request help on projects that may break laws, breach terms of services, or be considered malicious, inappropriate, or academically dishonest.**

Admins reserves the right to the following actions towards members who fail to comply:
• change nicknames of members
• remove rights, privileges, and roles of members
• discipline members (ban/kick/mute)

If you have any questions or would like some clarification to these rules, please contact any @⭐ E-Board member of Programming Club.
